Flo is a web application designed to create awareness about how we use energy. The topic of sustainability is a global issue that we are facing today more than ever due to our abundant use of non-renewable energy sources. In addition many are unaware of the various kinds of renewable energy sources that are available. Through flo, the aim is to create social awareness regarding energy consumption and the various energy sources used in modern times.
